Our story

Our company, Soft World, is which date back to 16 June 2025. Offers customers a range of soft toys and our main target customers are kids aged from 3-12. The industry that we are in is toy manufacturing and the company provides high quality, safe and emotionally comforting soft toys for children and collectors alike. As under the toy manufacturing industry all has a website to sell their toys, having a website is a competitive necessity to stay in competition with the other companies. This website will enable us to reach a wider market as it is not restricted to locals that only can buy in the retail store. This convenience will allow us to attract more customers. Reviews are more easily kept tracked as compared to retail, this allows us to improve on our product and services.
